The Cause

You got into this work because you care.

Today's most committed social sector leaders operate with great courage. Their organizations transform lives and are often the last resort for those left behind by the private or public sector.

These leaders know their clients best and they are experts in solving the problems those clients face. They aspire to create joyful and secure places of work for dedicated employees. They are ready to do whatever it takes to ensure longevity of the mission.

Society's most generous servants find themselves endlessly fighting fires, unable to focus on the very strategies that will optimize outcomes.

The Challenge

....but complex pressures make caring a lot more work.

Today nonprofit leaders face:

  • Staff burnout and difficulty retaining top talent
  • Increasing client needs with finite resources
  • Loss of reliable funding sources and donor uncertainty
  • Leadership isolation with no trusted thought partner
  • A lack of internal systems needed to strengthen efforts and scale impact

The result is you have passion without capacity, urgency without strategy, and a gap between what you know needs to happen and what you have the bandwidth to do.

The Solution

Most leaders already have the wisdom to meet this moment. Coaching helps them use it.

The truth is that most leaders already have the wisdom and knowledge they need to sustainably enact their missions. What they need is the space, reflection, and focus to translate that wisdom into concrete action plans—and the accountability to act on those plans with discipline.

After twelve years in the social sector as a coach, organizational developer, and training and development professional, I'm eager and qualified to support you in taking your passion, drive, and energy to the next level.

Coaching is not a set of recommendations or a diagnostic report. It is an ongoing relationship—one that creates the conditions for you to work on your organization's longevity, not just in it.
