About Ellie

Coaching rooted in twelve years of real-world experience

I've spent my career working inside the organizations nonprofits depend on—disability resource, addiction recovery, workforce development, community development, and education. That foundation informs everything I bring to a coaching relationship.

Across 3 countries and 8 nationalities, I've seen that the dreams driving people into nonprofit work are universal: we all seek relationships, security, and meaning. My job is to help you build an organization worthy of those dreams.

"Coaching is an ongoing relationship—not a set of recommendations or a diagnostic report."
